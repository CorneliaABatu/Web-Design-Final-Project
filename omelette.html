Veggie Omelette

Due to our increased number of healthy requests we bring to you the veggie omelette. We add mushrooms, spinach, onions, and peppers to this omelette and serve it with our homemade home fried potatoes which also include chopped veggies inside. The omelette can be made with eggwhites or egg beaters just let us know how you prefer your omelette. All our omelettes,inluding our veggie omelette, are served with toast. Your choices of toast include: white, whole wheat, multigrain, or rye toast.
