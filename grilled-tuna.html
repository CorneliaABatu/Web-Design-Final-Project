Grilled Tuna Steak

Our grilled tuna steak is cooked rare in a roasted pepper sauce. It is served with rice and potates but you can request it to come with any kind of side you'de like. For example, our sweet potatoe fries, roasted potatoes, mashed potatoes, baked potatoe, or sauteed mixed veggies. This is served with the soup of the day or a house salad...you pick!
