Rami's Famous Sizzilin' Shrimp Scampi

Our shrimp scampi is served with homemade linguine pasta in a sauteed extra virgin olive oil sauce with roasted garlic, herbs, and white wine. Rami our head chef serves the shrimp sizzilin' in your plate addin a Southern touch to this Italian style dish. You can request for the scampi to be served with other sides such as sweet potatoe french fries, mashed potatoes, baked potatoe, sauteed veggies, or roasted potatoes.
